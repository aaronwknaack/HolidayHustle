Yule Auction Extravaganza

Begin Game

Long ago, nestled deep in the icy heart of the North Pole, there was a magical treasure trove guarded by Santa's most trusted elves.

This vault contained an assortment of whimsical trinkets, rare treasures, and legendary artifacts collected from centuries of Christmas adventures.

Every 100 years, Santa declares a grand celebration known as the Yule Auction Extravaganza, where families from around the world are invited to compete in festive challenges and bid on the treasures of the vault. 🎁

The auction isn’t just about riches—it’s about wit, luck, and holiday cheer!

This year, your family has been chosen to partake in this legendary event. 🎉

Over five rounds, you'll bid on curious and enchanted items ✨, each with its own story and charm. But be warned—the elves are mischievous, and they've planned mini-games to test your holiday spirit and skill.

Only the cleverest and most spirited will claim victory in this ultimate Christmas quest!
